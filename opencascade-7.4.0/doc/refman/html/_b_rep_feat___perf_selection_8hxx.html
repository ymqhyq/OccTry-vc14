enum BRepFeat_PerfSelection

To declare the type of selection semantics for local operation Perform methods.

  • NoSelection
  • SelectionFU - selection of a face up to which a local operation will be performed
  • SelectionU - selection of a point up to which a local operation will be performed
  • SelectionSh - selection of a shape on which a local operation will be performed
  • SelectionShU - selection of a shape up to which a local operation will be performed.
